How to play

1. Choose Your Category:
Tap one of the buttons to select your move: name, animal, and country

2. Wait for the Guessing:
The computer will randomly pick a guess word.

3. Check Who Wins:
- Choose the letter
- If it is correct, go up
- If it is not correct, it is changed to a grey color, the number for guessing is less, and the part of the man is shown.
- if the total man is shown, so you lose
